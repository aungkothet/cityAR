  • CityAR- Smarter City with AR Tech

    Augmented reality is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real-world are enhanced by computer-generated perceptual information, sometimes across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory and olfactory.

    AR has captured the public imagination in the form of Pokémon games, Snapchat filters, Minecraft demos, and much more. However, applying this tech to a less sexy task—seamlessly integrating data into everyday experiences—could provide much more concrete value to cities.

  • Museum

    Our City-AR will be used at museum showing the attractive contents. AR can help visitors to understand historical events by making them appear in animated 3D. Visitors download an app and can then use the camera on their phone or tablet to explore the historical events. CityAR will also be available on web platform.

    Events

    Like Art Gallery Events, artist can't explain all of the audience one by one. We can help this problem. Visitors can download or use website to tell them about the detail of the art just like curation by artist plus can be seen as a attractive content.

    University Campus

    Getting around campuses can be extremely difficult to people who are new and do not know how to get around well. CityAR can be used for university campuses and would allow students and visitors to easily navigate around campus and remove the need for confusing maps.

    Airports

    Airports are mostly difficult to navigate, but our product removes the need for confusion, and enables travellers to navigate through them via a mobile app. Not only does CityAR make navigation at airports simpler, it also solves the common language barrier, especially at international airports.
